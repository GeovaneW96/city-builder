# Audio Design

## Purpose

Audio makes the city feel responsive and alive.

Even simple sounds can greatly improve perceived quality.

## Audio Goals

Audio should:

- confirm actions;
- reward milestones;
- signal problems;
- create ambient city life;
- avoid becoming annoying.

## Sound Effects

Recommended first sounds:

- road placement click;
- zone painting soft brush;
- building constructed;
- insufficient funds;
- warning alert;
- milestone reached;
- UI click;
- demolition.

## Ambient Audio

Ambient audio:

- quiet city ambience;
- traffic hum;
- birds/park ambience;
- industrial hum;
- rain/weather.

## Music

Recommended music direction:

- calm;
- optimistic;
- light strategy/management tone;
- not too repetitive.

Music should not distract from planning.

## Warning Audio

Warnings should be subtle.

Avoid loud repetitive alerts.

Use:

- one-time notification sound;
- visual persistent warning;
- optional mute setting.

## Audio Settings

Audio settings:

- master volume;
- music volume;
- SFX volume;
- mute;
- reduced alert sounds.
