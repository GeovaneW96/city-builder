# Monetization and Analytics

## Purpose

This document records monetization and analytics considerations.

Monetization is not in initial scope. Do not implement monetization unless explicitly decided.

## Monetization Principles

The game should avoid predatory mechanics.

Avoid:

- energy timers;
- pay-to-win;
- forced ads;
- manipulative daily streaks;
- intentionally slow progress to sell boosts.

Potential acceptable models:

- paid premium version;
- cosmetic packs;
- scenario packs;
- supporter edition;
- DLC-style expansions;
- optional donations.

## Analytics Goals

Analytics should help improve the game, not manipulate players.

Useful metrics:

- session length;
- tutorial completion rate;
- time to first road;
- time to first building;
- time to first warning;
- time to first bankruptcy;
- scenario completion rate;
- most common cause of failure;
- most ignored warning;
- most used building category.

## Analytics

Analytics should be privacy-friendly and local/dev-only if added.

## Product Questions to Answer

- Do players understand what to do first?
- Where do players get stuck?
- Is the first scenario too easy or too hard?
- Which warnings are confusing?
- Do players want to continue after 1,000 population?
- Which unlocks feel exciting?
